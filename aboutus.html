Get to Know Us

Our Story


Scent Sational is a proudly made in Malaysia artisanal, handmade fragrance company. In our sunny Kuala Lumpur studio, since 2016, our 100% premium soy candles are hand-poured in small batches. We value the craft of quality, and each carefully chosen aroma is manufactured with affection for individuals who value handcraftsmanship. Our handcrafted soy candles are built on a foundation of product development.

UNIQUE AND SUSTAINABLE

At Scent Sational, we value meticulous craftsmanship, premium materials, and environmentally-friendly practises. Our candles have a minimalist appearance and are gender-neutral. We take pride in developing a custom line of fragrances using the finest blend of premium fragrance oils, essential oils, and natural extracts that are produced in accordance with IFRA standards and are phthalate-free. Each fragrance has been carefully chosen and tested. To create the highest fragrance throw, each candle is scented to the fullest extent possible.

MINIMALISTIC

All it takes is one scent to bring back a thousand memories. Light up your day with wonderful scents. Built to serve as a constant reminder of our unforgettable journeys and encounters.


Our affordable, environmentally friendly candles, which are made with the highest-quality materials and presented in visually pleasing packaging, reflect an appreciation for simplicity, design, and purpose. We hope that your index will look lovely with our soy candles in it, that they will provide warmth to any setting, and that they will brighten your day. Scent Sational

Our soy candles are less prone to cause allergies and are less hazardous than paraffin candles. Additionally, because it burns at a lower temperature, soot—the dark substance you see in mass-produced candles—is less likely to remain in your candle as a byproduct.

Our soy candles also have a lower melting point that enables them to burn slower and cleaner than paraffin-based candles. Furthermore, unlike paraffin which comes from petroleum, soy is a renewable plant-derived product.
