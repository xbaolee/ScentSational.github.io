  1. Are your candles formulated using safe ingredients?
    All our candles are formulated using premium soy wax which are biodegradable, eco-friendly, and less likely to trigger allergies. We use a combination of fragrance and essential oils that are phthalate free and comply with IFRA (International Fragrance Association) standards. Our fragrance and wax are non-toxic, vegan and cruelty free. Pregnant mothers are advised to use their own discretion.
  2. Where do you source your materials from?
    We source from overseas suppliers to ensure our soy candles are up to standard.
  3. Are your products vegan?
    Soy wax is a great vegan alternative as most paraffin candles can be toxic for your health and the environment.
  4. Do you offer gift wrapping service?
    We do not offer gift wrapping service as we strive to be an eco-conscious company and play our part in reducing waste.
  5. Are your products halal/contain alcohol?
    All our candles are made from eco-friendly soy wax and blend of essential and fragrance oil. No alcohol or alcoholic beverage is included in the making of our candles. Our products do not carry a halal certification.

  8. What is the shelf-life of the candles?
    As our candles are hand-poured fresh in small batches and we do not use any preservatives, they have a shelf life of typically 9 -12 months. Keep the candles away from direct sunlight and store at room/cool temperature to prolong the shelf-life.
  9. Do you do custom scents?
    We are able to do custom scents given advance notice. Custom scents usually take up to 9 months. Minimum quantity applies. Please email to ScentSational@gmail.com for more information.

  11. How soon can I expect my order?
    Once an order is placed, please allow up to 1-2 business days for your order to be shipped out. We hand-pour each candle and the entire process is done manually by hand including pouring, bottling, labelling and shipping. Our hand-poured candles are crafted with love and will take a little longer to produce as compared to mass-produced candles. We also want to ensure that you receive freshly made candles for your olfactory experience to indulge.
